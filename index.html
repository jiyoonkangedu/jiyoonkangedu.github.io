About

Teacher, researcher, and drama educator.

My academic background spans primary education, elementary music education, drama education, and dramatic and arts-based research. I received my undergraduate degree in Primary Education with a concentration in Elementary Music Education, completed my master’s degree in Drama Education, and am currently pursuing doctoral study in Dramatic and Arts-Based Research.

Before beginning my doctoral studies, I taught elementary students in South Korea for four and a half years. I am currently in my second year of teaching at a community-based heritage language school in the U.S. Midwest.
