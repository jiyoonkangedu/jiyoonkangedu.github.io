About

I studied primary education (B.Ed., Elementary Music Education), drama education (M.Ed.), and now dramatic and arts-based research (Ph.D.).

Previously, I worked as an elementary school teacher in South Korea (4.6 years). Currently, I teach at a community-based heritage language school in the U.S. Midwest (2 years).
