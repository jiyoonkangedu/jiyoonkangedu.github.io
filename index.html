About

I studied primary education (B.Ed., Elementary Music Education), drama education (M.Ed.), and now dramatic and arts-based research (Ph.D.).

Previously, I taught elementary students in South Korea for four and a half years. Currently, I teach at a community-based heritage language school in the U.S. Midwest in my second year.
